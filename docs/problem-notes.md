## Observed / Reported Problem
Students and lecturers face a lack of real-time updates regarding sudden schedule changes or classroom relocations. This leads to missed classes, confusion, and wasted time commuting or waiting outside classrooms because updates are not synchronized or delievered instantly,

## Current Alternatives or Workarounds
| Workaround | Who Uses It? | Weakness / Pain | Evidence Source |
|Checking PDF timetables on the university website.|Students and Lecturers|The PDF is static and not updated in real-time when changes occur.| |
|Asking peers in class via Messenger/via LINE group.| Stsudents| Information gets buried quickly in chat history, leading to missed updates.|  |
|Checking physical notices about room changes on the classroom door | Lecturers and Administrative Staff | Students only find out after they physically arrive at the classroom, resulting in wasted time and confusion. | |
